**About the Organization**

BlueGrid is a leading vessel-to-grid company that turns marine vessels into virtual power plants for utility-grade energy storage. They work at the convergence of two net-zero megatrends: Electric Marine Transportation and Net Zero Electricity Grids. BlueGrid's mission is to create a sustainable path to net zero by reducing reliance on fossil fuels, creating energy resiliency for communities, and accelerating the adoption of electric vessels.
